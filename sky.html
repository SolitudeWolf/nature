Stars are synonymous with romance. At the edge of the Milky Way, 30,000 light-years from Earth, there are two huge galaxies with "tangoes" staged. The two galaxies are made up of billions of stars and clouds of gas, both spiraling in shape. The stars, gas and dust of the larger galaxies on the right form an "arm" that surrounds the smaller galaxies on the left, slowly posing in various graceful dances under the interaction.
